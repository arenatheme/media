Theme Styles

Change Homepage Styles

The settings_data.json file stores the theme settings data saved from the theme editor. It can also include ‘theme styles’, also known as presets.

MediaCenter support 2 Homepage Styles that you switch by replace presets settings_data.json.

  1. From your Shopify admin, go to Online Store > Themes.
  2. Find the theme you want to edit, and then click Actions > Edit code
  3. On the Edit HTML/CSS page, locate and click on the Config folder to reveal its content

  4. Replace current settings_data.json by the new one at pre-setting folder

    • Open setting_data.json in pre-settings folder. Click to Open.
    • Copy content and Paste it to settings_data.json in Web Browser.
    • Click Save.
