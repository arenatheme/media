Product Countdown Timer

Adding Countdown Timer to Product Page

You may following instruction about Shopify Metafield name key countdown_timer to add your short product description.

Date format is mm/dd/yyyy

Mass edit to add short description c_f.countdown_timer.

  1. Go to: https://shopify.com/admin/bulk?resource_name=Product&edit=metafields.c_f.c_f.countdown_timer:string
  2. Add product countdown timer for each product.
